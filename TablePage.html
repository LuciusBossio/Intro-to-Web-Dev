Organic Cannabis Pest Management

Pest Treatment

Common pests encountered while growing organic cannabis and various strategies for eliminating them.

 Refresh page to go back.
Pest Index
Type Pest Picture Description Treatment
Insects Aphids Very small, can be many colours, may have wings, oval shaped, can be found anywhere on the plant.
  • Amend soil with rice hulls
  • Apply foliar spray or soil drench containing citric acid or kaitenase
  • Change clothes before entering garden and have dedicated shoes
  • Do not allow other people or pests to enter garden
Mites Too small to see without magnification, use symptoms as a guide: droopy twisted leaves, curled leaf edges, twisted new growth, web (spider mites), yellowing or dead spots. Symptoms should be concentrated in specific areas.
  • Amend soil with rice hulls
  • Apply foliar spray or soil drench containing citric acid or kaitenase
  • Change clothes before entering garden and have dedicated shoes
  • Do not allow other people or pests to enter garden
  • Remove affected matter
Hard Bodied Insects Typically larger insects that consume large amounts of plant stem or foliage.
  • Amend soil with rice hulls
  • Apply foliar spray or soil drench containing citric acid or kaitenase
  • Keep plants indoors or cover with shade screen or a greenhouse
Flying Insects Small gnats or flies found around and on the underside of foliage.
  • Amend soil with rice hulls
  • Apply foliar spray or soil drench containing citric acid or kaitenase
 Mold/Fungus  Powdery Mildew White powdery substance on the surface of the plant.
  • Amend soil with rice hulls
  • Remove affected matter
  • Remove any aphid infestations
  • Manage VPD and reduce diurnal temperature swing
  • Do not over water
  • Do not allow plants to contact each other
Botryitis Dark brown/gray dead spots found inside flower.
  • Amend soil with rice hulls
  • Remove affected matter
  • Remove any aphid infestations
  • Manage VPD and reduce diurnal temperature swing
  • Do not over water
  • Do not allow plants to contact each other
Viruses Tobacco Mosaic Virus Symptoms include twisted and stunted growth, curled leaf tips, yellow spots or specks on unhealthy leaves, a lack of cannabinoid and terpene production. Found all over the plant.
  • Currently no organic cure
  • Amend soil with rice hulls
  • Sanitize pruning equipment and garden, maintain overall cleanliness
  • Avoid breaking cell walls and preventing other pests from doing so
